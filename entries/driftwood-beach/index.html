Exploring Driftwood Beach

Jan 31, 2024

We had a great time visiting scenic driftwood beach on beautiful Jekyll Island. It's on the atlantic coast and the sea has slowly pushed the beach back over what used to be forest. The result is a beach covered in dead trees and driftwood. Walking along the beach felt like walking through a forbidden forest of sorts, except the sun and sand and sea made it all so scerene.

Beautiful wispy clouds behind the trees

The kids enjoyed running to and from the "stump island". Needless to say Eames got soaked :). The tide was slowly going out so the island eventually connected to dry ground.
